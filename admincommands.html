Console Commands: (accessed by hitting ` )

Note: Below commands must include a '0' or '1' at the end (ie. 'AdminForceAllRoleAvailability 0' or 'AdminForceAllRoleAvailability 1')

AdminForceAllRoleAvailability 1: Allows players to choose whichever kit they want.

AdminDisableVehicleKitRequirement 1: Allows players to access vehicles that have kit requirements (Pilot / Crewman vehicles)

AdminDisableVehicleTeamRequirement 1: Allows players to access any vehicle, including enemy vehicles.
