ABOUT US

Welcome to the 1st Infantry Division! We are an international Mil-Sim light with all the tactics, structure, and skill of a mil-sim without the yes sir no sir hassle. Our mission is to be the best of the best while having the most fun along the way. We welcome anyone with a desire to play the game the right way with open arms. We are a mature, and understanding community with no mandatory attendance at training's or events. We run multiple community wide events and operations from 1 life events, death races, competetive matches against other teams, to internal 1ID scrims between Squads or against other units.

After a new recruit indicates they'd like to join on our Discord server, they will be scheduled for basic training. Basic training ensures all members have a basic understanding of the game and have an idea of what to expect when operating in 1ID. This training takes about 15 to 20 minutes. Afterwards recruits will be promoted and be considered a member of 1ID.

If you want to become a part of a 9man Squad where your Squad Leader and Fire Team Leaders direct you towards victory, join the Infantry regiment! You'll find yourself advancing towards a city with supporting armor, manuevering around a ridge to flank the enemy or even sitting in a logistics truck with your squadmates hoping your driver doesn't somehow get you all killed again. Every game is sure to consist of plenty of running, shooting and friendly banter in comms.

If rifles and RPGs aren't big enough toys for you, join the Armor regiment! Load up into a giant killing-machine where proper coordination between driver and gunner will ultimately decide your fate. The Armor regiment is responsible for protecting the Infantry and Aviation squads - don't let that enemy vehicle mow down your Infantry.. that's your job! Establish dominance over the enemy team and ultimately lead your team to victory.

If you're a pilot and want to earn your wings (and out rank the majority of clan members), join the Aviation regiment! Perhaps you're just tired of seeing all of those helicopters pilots flying towards the nearest tree like a moth to a flame. Not every pilot has the skills to quickly land with a helicopter full of logistics supplies and infantry near the frontlines. Pilots in the Aviation regiments will become J-hook landing masters, as well as landing troops and providing critical supplies for the Infantry and Armor squads.
